About Me

My Motivation

My main motivation is to help people. To see the joy on someone's face when they see their vision come to life is incredibly fufilling. I'm encouraged when I get to be a part of a client's creative proccess.

My Inspiration

My family! I'm blessed to have an incredible wife with whom I have a beautiful son! His sense of imagination combined with my wife's alternative view inspires me to keep creating and to continue to innovate.

My Education

In 2008, I graduated from The Audio Recoring Academy with a certificate in Audio Engineering, and recently graduated from Sheridan College from the Interactive Media Management program. The program was focused on Design and Development for both web & mobile.

My Aspiration

My dream is to have my media company, "Plunge Creative Media", become a fully staffed organization that wouold be developing and innovating for clients around the world.
